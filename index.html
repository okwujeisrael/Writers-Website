Cheryl Smith is a London-based writer from Nigeria. A keen reader of fiction, Cheryl enjoys prose steeped in emotional truth. She is particularly fond of Toni Morrison's work. In 2016, she won a scholarship to study creative writing at the prestigious University of East Anglia. Her work has appeared in such magazines as Litro, Saraba, Kalahari Review and East Jasmine Review. Cheryl is currently working on a novel set in the hilly town of Abeokuta, alongside short stories and occasional essays. In the daytime, she works as a copywriter and is a consummate cat lover.
